Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make sure I choose the right dumpster size and don't overpay?

The best way is to talk to our team. Give us a detailed description of your project. An experienced professional can almost always accurately recommend the correct roll off dumpster size, which is the key to getting a cheap roll off dumpster rental by not paying for space you don't need.

How do you calculate the final weight of my debris?

All of our trucks are required to go to a certified weigh station at the disposal facility. They weigh the truck when it's full (gross weight) and again after it's been emptied (tare weight). The difference between these two numbers is the exact, certified weight of your disposed material, a copy of which is available to you.

What is a "trip fee" and how do I avoid it?

A trip fee is a charge incurred if our driver arrives for a scheduled delivery or pickup and cannot complete the service due to an obstacle, like a parked car blocking the placement area. You can avoid it by ensuring the entire access path is clear and unobstructed on your service day.

Can the dumpster be placed on a city street in front of my business?

In most municipalities, placing a roll off dumpster bin on a public street requires a permit from the city's department of public works or a similar entity. You, as the renter, are typically responsible for securing this permit before we can schedule the delivery.

What materials are not allowed in dumpsters?

Hazardous waste, chemicals, asbestos, and certain electronics are prohibited. Our team provides a detailed list before delivery.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Rental periods are flexible, ranging from short-term use to extended projects. We’ll work with you to match your schedule.

Weed, CA Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Weed is a city in Siskiyou County, California, United States. As of the 2020 Census, the city had a total population of 2,862, down from 2,967 in 2010. There are several unincorporated communities adjacent to, or just outside, Weed proper, including Edgewood, Carrick, and Lake Shastina. These communities generally have mailing addresses that use Weed or its ZIP code. Weed is about 10 miles (16 km) west-northwest of Mount Shasta, a prominent northern California landmark, and the second-tallest volcano in the Cascade Range.
